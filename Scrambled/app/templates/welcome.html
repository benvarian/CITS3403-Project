Welcome to Scrambled!

The aim of Scrambled is simple - create the highest scoring words from 7 random letters

Every day a new set of random letters is generated.
Your goal is to form 6 words from these letters.
Letters may only be used once.

Login to compete with friends!

Enjoy our game!

Scrambled has two game modes - Normal and Speed. Each mode can be played daily, with different letters for each.
